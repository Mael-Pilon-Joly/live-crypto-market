Welcome to Crypto-Manager

Welcome to Crypto-Manager, your one-stop solution for managing and tracking cryptocurrency portfolios. Our platform provides a comprehensive set of tools and insights to help you stay ahead in the dynamic world of cryptocurrency trading. Below is an overview of the different sections of our application. Keep in mind that is it stricly a simulation. The data about the price of the different crypto assets come from Coin API, and we have observed unreliable value of a the price of the assets on a frequent basis. Furthermore, all usage of money on this website is fictional, no real money is involved.

Dashboard Overview

Visualizations

The graphs in the dashboard visualize the trends of cryptocurrency prices, highlighting both gains and losses.

Portfolio Management

Create Portfolio

Start by creating a new portfolio. Simply enter a name for your portfolio and click "Create Portfolio".

Load Data

Select a date range to load historical data related to your portfolio.

Your Portfolios

This section lists all your portfolios, showing the total worth, the amount spent, unrealized gains/losses, and the profit percentage.

Division of Cryptos (Partition)

A pie chart that visually represents the distribution of different cryptocurrencies in your selected portfolio.

Buy/Sell Cryptocurrency

Use this section to execute transactions. You can buy or sell cryptocurrencies based on the current market prices.

Getting Started

If you're new to the platform, start by creating a portfolio and exploring the different sections of the dashboard. Use the Buy/Sell functionality to manage your assets, and keep an eye on the dashboard to stay updated on market trends and your portfolio's performance.
